# AI Report Writer – Product Overview

AI Report Writer combines AI-generated narratives with customizable templates so teachers can produce quality feedback faster. Key highlights:

- **Personalized reports** that adapt to each student’s performance and goals.
- **Template Builder & Manager** that let educators reuse and refine best-practice frameworks.
- **Usage tracking** to monitor monthly credits and make sure everyone stays within their plan.

Access all the live tools at https://aireportwriter.app/.
